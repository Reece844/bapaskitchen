> 1/4 cup	brown sugar

> 1/2 tsp	ginger

> 2 cloves	chopped garlic

> 1/2 cup	soy sauce

> 1/4 cup	water

> 2 Tbsp	oil

> 1/2 tsp	pepper


Mix all ingredients together and put inside a ziplock bag with meat. Let it marinate overnight or longer.
